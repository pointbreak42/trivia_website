Help

How to Play

  1. Click Play on the home page to start a new trivia game.
  2. Choose your category (if available) and answer each question by clicking the correct option.
  3. Each game consists of 5 questions. Try to answer as many as you can correctly!
  4. After the game, view your score and see how you rank on the leaderboard.
  5. Use the sidebar to navigate to your profile, settings, or other pages at any time.

Feature Overview

  • Home: Start a new game and access main navigation.
  • Profile: View your username and top score.
  • Leaderboard: See the top scores and compete with others.
  • Settings: Toggle dark mode, control music, and reset your progress.
  • About: Learn more about Brain Blast and its creators.
  • Help: Find instructions and tips for using the site.
